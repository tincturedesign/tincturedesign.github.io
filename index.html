What we do

WEB

We work with clients to build beautiful responsive sites that are guaranteed to turn heads. Instead of charging a large amount up front, we have clients pay monthly, while we maintain their online presence and keep them up to date.

SOCIAL

We offer Social Media branding and marketing in our monthly service. When combined with our web design, custom social can really boost a business's online presence.

CUSTOM DESIGN

All of our service comes with a promise of quality design based off of the core ideals and values of the client and their business. Designing the ultimate digital experience is our passion.

DESIGN

Nate Hicks is a Designer & Creative Problem Solver. He has worked in all areas of Graphic Design, Web Design, and UI Design and can adapt these skills to any project.

Why hello there! My name is Nate and I have an undying passion for all things aesthetic. I specialize in graphic design, branding, web design and UI design. I've worked in corporate, agency, and freelance environments and have a knack for staying adaptable. I received my BFA in Graphic Design from Montclair State University and have nearly 10 years of experience in the field with multiple certifications in Web and Interactive Design.

With my experience as a designer, I've come to realize that the client and the user come first and foremost. A designer is expected to use their skills wisely, but also get in touch with the client's idea, and the data that the design pulls in from users. This creates a perfect ecosystem, where design can grow and evolve naturally to keep up with design trends and best practices. Working with Eric’s bulletproof coding, we we can build beautiful digital media quickly and efficiently.

DEVELOPMENT

Eric Barber is a Full Stack Developer with a focus on UX. He has worked with small firms & large corporations alike, developing everything from websites to complete web applications.

On the development side of the house, I'm Eric. My focus is on the latest technologies and frameworks of all things front-end. Professionally speaking, I'm a full-stack engineer that utilizes design and unique UX to create everything from highly technical applications to simple single page sites. My experience consists of working at start-ups, agencies, remote companies, and large corporations for 5 years. I attended Fairleigh Dickinson University in Teaneck, NJ to obtain a BS in Information Technology with a Minor in Fine Arts.

As a developer, I've come to navigate the complete 'stack' – from planning, meeting, and discussing needs with clients and users, to developing, and leading teams. I don't settle for mediocre quality. I pour blood, sweat, and tears into each application and provide support when needed. Combined with Nate's expertise in design, we aim to provide functional and aesthetic sites to small to large clients in a timely manner. We bridge the gap between cutting edge design and next generation development.
